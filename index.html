Inlet

Feed Pressure at inlet is the minimum inlet pressure required at the high pressure pump inlet, pressure must be measured at the inlet port.

Larger units have a designated pressure switch port.

When sizing a suitable boost pump, we recommend a margin of 25% to allow for inlet filter and pipework losses.

All inlet water must be filtered to 5µm

NB – If the inlet pressure drops below the minimum level the unit must be stopped immediately.
